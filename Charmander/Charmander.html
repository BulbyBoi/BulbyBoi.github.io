Charmander

The Lizard Pokemon

Charmander (ヒトカゲ) #0004

Charmander was designed by Atsuko Nishida, one of the character designers for Pocket Monsters Red and Blue.

It first served as the first generation games' fire starter Pokemon (Red and Blue). Its japanese name Hitokage literally means salamander. It has prominent reptillian appearance and its iconic final evolution sports a draconic look. Both it and its final evolution shares the same color. However, Charmeleon, its next form, has a darker shade.

Appearance

  1. Primary Appearance: It has a medium-sized body, short legs, and a long, tapering tail.
  2. Color: It is primarily orange with. However, it does have a cream-colored underside. Some official depictions show Charmander with blue or black eyes.
  3. Flaming tail: The most distinctive feature of Charmander is its tail. It slowly tapers until the flame of its tip. Whether or not Charmander actually dies with its flame is not necessarily known.

Charmander has a more childish appearance which slowly transforms into a more draconic look as it evolves.
